# A+ Content v2 — Extra Large Print Sudoku Puzzle Book

**Book:** `extra_large_print_sudoku` (id 8) · BrainCraft Publishing · 100 puzzles · 128 pages · $9.99
**Audience:** seniors + low-vision adults (60+, post-cataract, macular degeneration, glaucoma)
**USP:** 30-pt numerals · 1 puzzle / page · hand-verified unique solutions · 8.5×11 large format

## 1 · Why this redesign

Competitor benchmark — **B0FFZ9K9CG** (Mind & Logic Books, #1 BSR Combinatorics, 4.5★, 41 reviews):

| Element | Competitor | Our v1 (current) |
|---|---|---|
| Background | Dark grid texture, premium feel | Soft sage gradient — too "cozy" |
| Headline | Huge yellow bold on dark | White on green, smaller |
| 3D mockup | Cover floats, hard drop-shadow, perspective | Flat crop, light shadow |
| Verified badge | Yellow circular stamp, prominent | Not present |
| Difficulty visual | 3 colored cards (green/yellow/red) with grid samples | 4 small icons in 2×2 grid |
| Interior preview | Open-book mockup + 3 checkmarks list | 3 fan-spread cards, no checkmark list |
| Manual verification | Cartoon illustration "Too many solutions vs Solved" | Not present |
| Brain/Gift CTA | 3 colored columns with icons (brain / badge / gift) | 4-column scenario row, smaller icons |

**Bottom line:** competitor closes harder because every module screams ONE benefit with high-contrast typography + a memorable visual. Our v1 is too text-heavy and the watermark digits dilute attention.

## 2 · Color & typography refresh

**Keep** the green scheme — it matches our cover (`#1B5E3F` dark forest) — but increase contrast and add motion.

| Token | v1 | v2 |
|---|---|---|
| Headline color | `#FFFFFF` (white) | `#F5C842` (yellow gold) — pops on dark green |
| Headline weight | 38–56pt | 56–72pt, ALL CAPS, tight tracking |
| Body color | `#FFFFFF` | `#FFFFFF` (unchanged) |
| Background texture | Random 0.40 DPI digits, density 25 | **Sudoku grid lines** (1px white @ 8% opacity, 9×9 macro grid) overlaid on dark green |
| Accent stripe | 12px ribbon top edge | 18px gradient ribbon (red→gold) |
| Drop shadow on mockups | 8px black 60α | 24px black 80α + 4px outer glow yellow 30α |

## 4 · Conversion uplift hypothesis

| Element | Mechanism | Expected lift |
|---|---|---|
| Yellow bold hero headline | Reverses contrast, doubles readability | +15% scroll-through |
| 3D mockup with hard shadow | "Real product" cue vs flat artwork | +8% trust |
| `VERIFIED BY HAND` stamp | Reduces "AI slop" objection (top 1★ complaint in category) | +10% conversion |
| 3 colored difficulty cards | Visual progression — viewer self-selects "I'm a Medium" → buys | +12% add-to-cart |
| Module 5b (hand-verified visual) | Differentiates from competitor at the bottom of the funnel | +5% on the fence buyers |
| 3-col Brain/Eyes/Gift | Multi-occasion framing → expands TAM (gift buyers = 30-40% of category) | +15% gift purchases |

Combined hypothesis: **+25–40% conversion vs v1 layout**, justifies the render cost.
