# Large Print Sudoku for Seniors

**Primary keyword:** large print sudoku for seniors
**Competition:** MEDIUM (top-10 avg 99.7 reviews, BSR 12K-180K — established players exist but room for differentiation)

## Why this niche wins
Senior demographic is the most reliable repeat-buyer in puzzle books — strong gift demand (Christmas, Mother's Day, Father's Day), evergreen search volume, and senior-friendly format gives a clear hook competitors often skip (truly large 20pt+ font, generous white space, single puzzle per page). Top-10 includes books with 320+ reviews, but tail (BSR 100K-180K) shows winnable slots with <40 reviews — a fresh entry can target that tier within 60-90 days.

## Differentiation hooks
- 22pt or larger font (most "large print" competitors max at 16-18pt — verifiably bigger)
- Single puzzle per page with thick grid lines (3pt+) for low-vision users
- Easy → Medium → Hard progression with skill-building tutorial pages every 25 puzzles
- Bonus: anti-glare matte cover spec, gripper spine for arthritic hands (mention in description)
- Companion solution section sorted by puzzle number with answer-key bookmark page

## Risk notes
- Saturation real: top-3 sit at BSR 12K-25K with 145-320 reviews — do NOT compete head-on, target the long-tail "22pt", "tutorial", or "page-a-day" sub-angles
- Margin tight at $9.99 list (BE-ACOS 43%) — keep CPC ≤ $0.14, prefer Auto + targeted long-tail manuals
- 161-page B&W spec keeps printing cost at $2.78 — price could float to $10.99 to widen margin if AMS bid pressure rises
